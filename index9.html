Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how Nexus Browser ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies. By using our website and services, you consent to the use of cookies as described.

🍪 Our Approach: We use minimal, privacy-respecting cookies. No tracking cookies are used for advertising. You can manage preferences below.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, and provide analytics. Nexus Browser uses cookies only for essential functionality and anonymous analytics.

Types of Cookies We Use

TypePurposeDurationEssential?
Essential CookiesAuthentication, security, and basic functionalitySession / 30 daysYes

Analytics CookiesAnonymous usage statistics (Plausible - no personal data)30 daysOptional

Cookie Details

  • nexus_session: Essential. Maintains your login session. Expires after 30 days.
  • nexus_preferences: Stores your settings (VPN, theme, default language). Expires after 1 year.
  • nexus_analytics_id: Anonymous identifier for usage statistics. No personal data. Expires after 30 days.
  • cookie_consent: Records your cookie preferences. Expires after 1 year.

Third-Party Cookies

We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies. However, if you install third-party extensions through our marketplace, those extensions may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing permissions before installation.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via settings
  • Our Preference Center: Use the buttons below to adjust your preferences
  • Do Not Track (DNT): We respect DNT signals from your browser
